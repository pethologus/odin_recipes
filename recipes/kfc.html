Korean Fried Chicken recipe


For this recipe you will need


Chicken:

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 70 g plain flour
  • 220 g cornflour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1000 g chicken wings

Sauce:

  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 50 g light brown sugar
  • 60 g rice vinegar
  • 60 g runny honey
  • 30 - 50 g Korean chilli paste (gochujang), to taste
  • 30 g tomato ketchup
  • 15 g dark soy sauce
  • 15 g sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds, for sprinkling
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced (5 mm), for sprinkling

Sauce preparation instructions:

To prepare sauce please follow the following steps

  1. Place garlic and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. 1Add flour, cornflour, baking powder, salt and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a large bowl then place bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in chicken wings then stir to coat. Cover with reusable wrap or cling film and place in fridge for 2-4 hours. Towards the end of this time, clean mixing bowl and make sauce.

To prepare sauce please follow the following steps

  1. Place garlic and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sugar, vinegar, honey, Korean chilli paste, ketchup, soy sauce and sesame oil then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, heat 10 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to a large bowl and keep warm.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep saucepan or deep fat fryer to 190°C (see tip).
  4. Fry chicken wings in batches of 4-5 for 12-15 minutes until all chicken is cooked. Transfer to a baking dish lined with paper towels and keep warm in low oven while frying remaining chicken.
  5. Transfer chicken to bowl with sauce and toss to coat. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and spring onions then serve hot.
